Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation emphasize a digitally empowered economy, leveraging technology for inclusive growth, and fostering a culture of entrepreneurship.

Sustainability

Focus on environmental resilience through renewable energy and sustainable practices, social inclusivity by ensuring equitable access to resources.

Infrastructure

Emphasize developing smart, sustainable urban planning, robust digital connectivity, and modern transportation systems.

Empowerment

Focus on promoting education, skill development, and gender equality to create opportunities for all.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh is not a small country of limited resources, it is a big nation of endless possibilities. Our strength lies in our people, our dreams, and our determination. Let us rise above our differences and build a nation that is inclusive, sustainable, and prosperous for all".

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
