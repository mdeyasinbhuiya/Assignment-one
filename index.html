Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation emphasize a digitally empowered economy, leveraging technology for inclusive growth, and fostering a culture of entrepreneurship.

Sustainability

Focus on environmental resilience through renewable energy and sustainable practices, social inclusivity by ensuring equitable access to resources.

Infrastructure

Emphasize developing smart, sustainable urban planning, robust digital connectivity, and modern transportation systems.

Empowerment

Focus on promoting education, skill development, and gender equality to create opportunities for all.
